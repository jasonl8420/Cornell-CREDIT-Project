About CREDIT

Founded in 2020 under the leadership of six undergraduate students, the Cornell Real Estate Development & Investment Trust ("CREDIT") has become the leading real estate organization at Cornell University. Since its inception, CREDIT has provided students with an experiential outlet to explore the real estate industry  through an education program, corporate partners, professional speakers, and practical investments.

Opportunities

Members accepted to CREDIT every recruitment cycle. Small recruitment classes:

  • create cohesive learning environments
  • enhance attention given to each new member
  • promote an intimate community

Active corporate partners that have established relationships with CREDIT. Functions of the partnership include:

  • researching real properties and markets
  • building new job opportunities
  • creating networks with industry professionals

Of real estate assets in which CREDIT research, analysis, and reports have functioned as the foundation of the capital-decision making. Activities include:

  • sub-market assessments
  • acquisition due diligence
  • financial modeling
  • recapitalization analysis
  • lease revenue management

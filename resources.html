Common Real Estate Terms

Real Property
land, and that which is “attached” to the land
Personal Property
everything other than real property
Real Estate
is real property consisting of land and the buildings (structures) that are affixed to it, along with the natural resources within and on the land.
Freehold Estate
  • Seisin – possession of land by freehold
  • Three Types
  • Think ownership
Non-Freehold Estate
  • No Seisin – no possession of land by freehold
  • Three Types
  • Think lease
